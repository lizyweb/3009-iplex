About Our Company

iPlex Technologies stands out as a leading Enterprise Solution Provider Company, headquartered in the vibrant cities of Mumbai and Chennai, India. With a dynamic presence in the market, iPlex is dedicated to offering a comprehensive suite of Business Software solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of small and mid-sized entities.


At the core of iPlex's offerings are robust and cutting-edge solutions, including but not limited to Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Point of Sale (POS), and Human Resources & Payroll Software. iPlex extends its expertise across a multitude of industry segments, showcasing its versatility in catering to Manufacturing, Healthcare, Textiles, Food Processing, Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Retail, Educational Institutes, Restaurants, and more.

Beyond these essential business software applications, iPlex goes the extra mile to provide an array of specialized solutions and services. The company offers a seamless SMS Gateway Solution, ensuring effective communication strategies. iPlex also excels in Real Estate and Property Management Solutions, Retail Solutions, Website Development, Mobile Application Development, E-commerce solutions, and a sophisticated Document Management System.
